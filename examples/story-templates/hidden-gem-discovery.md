# NFT Showcase Story Template

## Template: "Hidden Gem Discovery"

### Structure (60 seconds)

**Act 1: Hook (0-5s)**
- Flash of rare NFT with dramatic music
- Text overlay: "This NFT Changed Everything"

**Act 2: Context (5-20s)**
- Introduce the collection
- Show floor price and volume

**Act 3: The Showcase (20-45s)**
- Full reveal of featured NFT
- Unique trait analysis
- Rarity score visualization

**Act 4: Call-to-Action (45-60s)**
- Current pricing and where to buy
- "Follow for more NFT gems"

### Visual Style: Cinematic Rose Gold

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Rose Gold (#C9A075)
- Secondary: Deep Purple (#2D1B42)
- Accent: Bright Gold (#FFD700)

### Music Sync

**ScrollSoul Track:** Cinematic Electronic, BPM 120-130

**Beat Sync Points:**
- 0:00 - Opening impact
- 0:20 - Main reveal
- 0:45 - Call-to-action
