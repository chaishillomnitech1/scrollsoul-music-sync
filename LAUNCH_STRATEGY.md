## ðŸš¨ Risk Mitigation & Contingencies

### Potential Risks

**Risk 1: Low trial conversions**
- **Mitigation:** A/B test landing pages, improve onboarding
- **Contingency:** Offer extended trials, white-glove setup

**Risk 2: Technical issues at scale**
- **Mitigation:** Load testing, redundancy, monitoring
- **Contingency:** Rollback procedures, status page, proactive communication

**Risk 3: Negative PR**
- **Mitigation:** PR crisis plan, spokesperson training
- **Contingency:** Rapid response team, transparent communication

**Risk 4: Competitor response**
- **Mitigation:** Continuous innovation, customer lock-in
- **Contingency:** Competitive differentiation, customer retention program

**Risk 5: Regulatory changes (crypto)**
- **Mitigation:** Legal monitoring, compliance team
- **Contingency:** Fiat rewards alternative ready
