## üì¢ Marketing Channels

### 1. Content Marketing (Priority: High)

**Blog:**
- Frequency: 3x per week (Mon, Wed, Fri)
- Topics: Music licensing, crypto rewards, API integration, case studies
- Length: 1,500-3,000 words
- SEO-optimized with keywords
- Distribution: Website, Medium, LinkedIn

**Video:**
- Frequency: 2x per week (Tue, Thu)
- Types: Product demos, tutorials, customer interviews, thought leadership
- Length: 3-10 minutes
- Platforms: YouTube, LinkedIn, Twitter, Website

**Whitepapers:**
- Frequency: 1 per quarter
- Topics: Industry research, technical architecture, compliance guides
- Length: 15-30 pages
- Gated content for lead generation

**Case Studies:**
- Frequency: 1 per month
- Format: PDF + web page + video
- Length: 5-8 pages
- Distribution: Sales team, website, social media

**Podcasts:**
- Guest appearances: 2 per month
- Target podcasts: SaaS, HR tech, crypto, music industry
- Host own podcast (future): Monthly starting Q3 2024

### 2. Social Media (Priority: High)

**LinkedIn (Primary Channel):**
- Frequency: 5x per week
- Content mix: 40% thought leadership, 30% customer success, 20% product, 10% culture
- Target: B2B decision makers
- Paid ads: $60K/month budget

**Twitter (Developer Community):**
- Frequency: 3-5x per day
- Content mix: 50% product/technical, 30% industry news, 20% engagement
- Target: Developers, tech community
- Paid ads: $25K/month budget

**YouTube (Educational):**
- Frequency: 2x per week
- Content: Tutorials, demos, webinars, interviews
- Target: All audiences
- SEO optimization for discovery

**Medium (Thought Leadership):**
- Frequency: 1x per week
- Content: Long-form articles, industry insights
- Target: Industry professionals
- Cross-post from blog

**Reddit (Community Engagement):**
- Frequency: 2-3x per week
- Subreddits: r/webdev, r/saas, r/cryptocurrency
- Content: Helpful contributions, AMAs

## ‚úÖ Best Practices

### Content Creation
- Focus on quality over quantity
- Use data and specific metrics
- Include customer stories
- Optimize for SEO
- Make it scannable (headers, bullets, images)

### Social Media
- Visual content performs best
- Video gets 5x more engagement
- Post consistently
- Engage with comments
- Use analytics to optimize

### Email Marketing
- Personalize (name, company, industry)
- A/B test subject lines
- Mobile-optimized design
- Clear single CTA
- Segment your audience

### Paid Advertising
- Start with small budgets
- Test multiple variations
- Track conversions, not just clicks
- Retarget website visitors
- Use negative keywords

### SEO
- Create valuable content
- Build quality backlinks
- Optimize for user intent
- Monitor and adapt
- Patient long-term approach
